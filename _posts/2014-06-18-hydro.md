# Hydrographic surveys

Survey of submarine detail of up to 185m below water surface of medium sized waterways utilising 
echo sounders coupled to GPS receivers.(Mainly in-land waterways)

Applications in this regard include the modelling of reservoir dam basins to determine water 
capacities and silting levels and in the case of slimes dams, 
the deposition capacities for planning purposes.
